Chicken Biryani Reference

Description

Delcious chicken biryani, a culinary masterpiece of South Asia. Here's a basic rundown of how to make it:

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Fresh giner root
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Ground Cumin
  • Chili Powder
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Yogurt
  • Turmeric
  • Fresh Mint Leaves
  • Ground Cardamom
  • Cinnamon stick

Steps

  1. Buy Rice and Chicken
  2. Cook Rice and Chicken
  3. Cut everything else into pieces
  4. Cook it
  5. Mix it with rice and chicken
  6. Eat
